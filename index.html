Munich Olympic Park

The Olympic Park in Munich was used in 1972 and is now a tourist attraction; these Olympics games were also the site of a massacre when a military group kidnapped and executed Israeli athletes. The stadium and surrounding area are still used for many events, including farmers markets and concerts. The park is listed as a Unesco World Heritage site.
